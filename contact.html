Before you go

For a smooth Shanghai trip, prepare a simple plan before arrival. It is also helpful to save Chinese names of attractions on your phone.

  • Check opening hours before visiting museums.
  • Book popular attractions early during holidays.
  • Carry a portable charger for maps and translation apps.

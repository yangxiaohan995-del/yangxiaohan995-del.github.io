Why visit Shanghai?

Shanghai is one of China’s most recognizable cities because it combines riverfront architecture, modern business districts, historical neighborhoods, and a strong local food culture. The city is also easy to navigate by metro, which makes it friendly for short trips.

This website is designed as a compact guide. It does not try to list every attraction. Instead, it focuses on places and experiences that help a visitor understand the city quickly.

  • Best for first-time visitors and short stays.
  • Includes attractions, food, and a sample itinerary.
  • Uses simple travel logic: morning sights, afternoon walks, evening views.

Tip: The skyline looks most dramatic near sunset, especially from the Bund or across the Huangpu River.

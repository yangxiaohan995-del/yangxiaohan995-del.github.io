Five-Day Shanghai Itinerary

This sample itinerary is designed for a first-time visitor. It balances famous landmarks, slower neighborhood walks, food experiences, and one optional theme park day.

Suggested Schedule

Day Morning Afternoon Evening
Day 1 Yu Garden and old city area Nanjing Road walk The Bund night view
Day 2 Shanghai Museum or People’s Square Former French Concession Xintiandi dinner
Day 3 Lujiazui towers Riverside walk Huangpu River cruise
Day 4 Local breakfast tour Tianzifang or Wukang Road Local Shanghainese restaurant
Day 5 Shanghai Disney or Zhujiajiao Continue full-day activity Relaxed final dinner

Transportation Tips

  1. Use the metro for most major attractions.
  2. Choose one neighborhood per half day to avoid rushing.
  3. Leave extra time for evening views because the Bund can be crowded.

Budget Reminder

Shanghai can be either budget-friendly or expensive depending on hotels and restaurants. A balanced plan uses metro transport, local breakfast, and one or two higher-end meals.
